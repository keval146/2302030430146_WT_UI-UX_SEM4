Frequently Asked Questions

Discover more about Meal Planner Genius

How does Meal Planner Genius work?

Meal Planner Genius is an AI-powered solution that creates personalized meal plans based on your dietary preferences and restrictions. Simply answer a few questions, and our algorithm will generate a plan tailored to your unique needs and goals.

What does the meal plan include?

Your meal plan will be provided in a downloadable PDF format. It includes detailed information such as calorie counts, ingredient amounts, and a breakdown of meals for each day.

Does Meal Planner Genius include calorie count?

Meal Planner Genius includes detailed calorie counts for each meal, allowing you to easily track your nutritional intake and stay on top of your health and wellness goals.

Can I customize my meal plan?

Yes, customization is a breeze with Meal Planner Genius. You can easily adjust your meal plan to suit your evolving preferences and goals. Our solution covers all diet types, so you can make it work for you.
